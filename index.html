Digital Currency Development

This year, the total market capitalization of cryptocurrencies has increased over twenty times since the start of year. Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies have grown to become an acknowledged part of the financial system, albeit a new one.

What factors have contributed to the explosive growth of cryptocurrencies in 2017?
The short answers is nobody knows exactly. Part of the growth in cryptocurrencies value is event driven, while part of it can be explained by valuation formula of network effect. This project aims to visually present these theories.

Market Capitalization

Market Capitalization is a measure of cryptocurrency value. It is calculated by multiplying the volume of a cryptocurrency and current market price.



Metcalfe's Law

Metcalfe's law states that the value of a telecommunication network is proportional to the square of the number of connected users in the system. Based on that, Tommy Lee, a financial advisor suggested a simple model valuing bitcoin as the square function number of unique Bitcoin addresses times the average transaction value.

This model worked really well at the time, but has derailed from reality right after. Currently, It is capable of explaining 81.3% of all historical growth.





The rest of the growth is event-driven

Events in goverment regulation, technology, and Institution money accumulates the confidence for Bitcoin's growth.
